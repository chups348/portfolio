About Me

My name is Sara Chupa, and I’m currently pursuing a degree in Computer Programming and Analysis. With a focus on mastering various programming languages, I'm on a journey to become a full stack developer. I’m enthusiastic about building comprehensive solutions and always eager to explore new technologies and techniques.

I am proficient in Java, C++, C#, Python, SQL, React, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I have experience with Arduino IDE and am skilled in using Adobe Illustrator, Figma, and other Adobe design tools. Additionally, I work with Kali Linux and the Linux operating system.

Notable projects I’ve worked on include developing professional websites for Barney's and The Ceeps, two of the biggest bars in London, Ontario. These projects involved CSS, HTML, JavaScript, and Figma. I am currently planning to convert one of these projects into a React web app.

Outside of programming, I enjoy working with Arduino to build projects like drones, mechanical arms, and proximity sensors. I also enjoy reading, running, art, and participating in programming competitions and hackathons.
